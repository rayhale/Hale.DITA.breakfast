# Vegan Breakfast Smoothie

Makes 1 glass
##Ingredients
* 1 cup soy milk
* 1 ripe banana, sliced
* 1 cup favorite frozen fruit medley
* 1-2 tablespoons chia seeds
* 1 teaspoon powdered ginger

##Directions
